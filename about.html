Our Story

Demystifying Finance,
One Learner At A Time.

We believe that financial literacy is the ultimate equalizer. At Dixit Training, we exist to bridge the gap between complex financial concepts and everyday professionals.

Our Mission

"Making finance accessible to everyone."

We are on a mission to strip away the intimidating jargon and endless spreadsheets associated with finance. We replace it with clear frameworks, visual tools, and real-world logic so you can confidently steer your career or business.

Our Vision

"Financial literacy in every household."

Imagine a world where small business owners don't fear tax season, where employees understand their company's stock options, and where young adults know how to invest. That is the world we are building, one class at a time.

The Story Behind
Dixit Training.

"After spending over a decade as a financial consultant in Pune, I noticed a recurring, painful trend. Brilliant founders, exceptional marketers, and highly skilled engineers were completely freezing up when it came to understanding their own money."

They weren't failing because they lacked intelligence; they were struggling because the financial industry gatekeeps knowledge behind complicated acronyms and unnecessary math.

I started Dixit Training in a small room in Kothrud with a simple promise: If I can't explain a financial concept to you on a single piece of paper, I haven't understood it well enough myself.

Today, we've trained over 5,000 students and professionals. We don't just teach you how to read a balance sheet; we teach you how to use it as a weapon to grow your business and secure your future."

Our Journey.

How we grew from a small workshop to a premier training institute.

2015

The Beginning

Dixit Training was officially founded in Kothrud, Pune. We hosted our first "Finance for Non-Finance" offline workshop with just 12 students in attendance.

2018

The 1,000 Student Milestone

Through pure word-of-mouth, our flagship P&L simplification course reached over 1,000 enrollments. We expanded our team and introduced personal finance modules.

2021

Corporate Expansion

Started partnering with IT firms and startups across India to train their management teams on capital efficiency and cash flow management.

2026

The New Era

Launching our comprehensive digital ecosystem to bring our simplified financial frameworks to a global audience. The mission continues.
